our Mission

        We connect people around the world in the fight to end 
        poverty. Working together, we invest in the lives of 
        childrenand youth, build the healthy environments they
        need  to thrive, and empower them to create lasting
        change in their own lives and communities.
        

Your support makes this possible even amid a global crisis.
